# GardAI: Robotics and Artificial Intelligence for High Social Value

In the spirit of the National Digital School Plan, this informative meeting focused on the application of robotics and Artificial Intelligence in contexts of high social value. The event featured presentations on intelligent surgical robots, educational robotics, and a practical demonstration of Martian robotics with ROSITA (ITAlian Space ROver).

**Date:** April 07, 2022

**Location:** Centro Congressi di Garda, Garda (VR)

**Sponsors:** National Digital School Plan
